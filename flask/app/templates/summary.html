Browse > People's Daily 人民日报

People's Daily is the official newspaper of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), published worldwide with a circulation of 3 to 4 million. In addition to its main Chinese-language edition, it has editions in English, Japanese, French, Spanish, Russian, Arabic and Korean. People's Daily provides direct information on the policies and viewpoints of the Party.
