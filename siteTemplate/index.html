Keep Journalism Accountable

What is Newshub

Newshub is a tool that tracks changes made to online news stories in real time. Our goal in creating Newshub was to expose inappropriate alterations made in stories post-publication and censorship by governments. In this way, Newshub engages readers in helping keep journalism accountable.

How does Newshub work

Newshub tracks over 50 websites in Asian countries including China, Singapore and India. You can either browse alternations by publications or search for the alteration history of any article by its url.

More to come:

We produce monthly reports and visualizations to help our readers better understand the data that we collect. Monthly reports will highlight publications that make major changes to their online articles with both stories and visualizations. We will also write individual stories on substantial changes related to important news events.
